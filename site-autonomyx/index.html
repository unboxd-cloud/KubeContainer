Introduction

An agentic enterprise is one where work is federated across humans and agents under contract — every member declared, every capability bounded, every decision carrying its evidence. OpenAutonomyx is the canonical system for that work: the records are canonical, the agents are accountable, and trust is not a promise but a property of the record. No trust without evidence; no evidence without provenance.
