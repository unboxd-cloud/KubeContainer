5. research

Research

This folder contains research notes, references, examples, and working analysis for the Publishing Platform.

Purpose

Use this space to explore ideas before they become settled architecture or implementation. 

research/ = exploration, references, notes
docs/     = settled decisions and specifications
packages/ = implementation
apps/     = product surfaces

Research Areas

  • CMS architecture
  • Professional publishing workflows
  • Schema.org and JSON-LD
  • OpenAPI and MCP
  • RSS, Atom, and JSON Feed
  • Open Location Code / Plus Codes
  • Multilingual publishing
  • Location-aware publishing
  • Time-aware publishing
  • SEO and discovery
  • Editorial layout patterns
  • Multi-tenant content management

Rule

Research can be rough. Docs should be clear and actionable.
